NOTHING WORKS

Berlin underground band since 2022.

BIO

Formed in late 2022, Nothing Works is a rising force from Berlin’s underground subcultures. Blending '90s indie/alternative with modern melodic and post-hardcore, their sound—raw, urgent, and emotionally charged—can be summed up as “heavy melodic hardcore.”

Think Drug Church meets Bikini Kill — that’s a good starting point for understanding their unapologetically political lyrics and the fierce energy they bring to every show.

Their debut EP, "Bold Talk for a Burner Account", is set for release in June 2025, featuring four songs that give a clear sense of what they’re about. Embrace for impact!

The band, composed entirely of FLINTA* members, is quickly becoming a standout in the local scene, known for both their message and their momentum. Despite being young, Nothing Works has already supported notable acts from Austria, Canada, Germany, Mexico and the USA — including CURB, PUNITIVE DAMAGE, YEAHRS, SHORELINE, GIRLS GO SKA, GO! NYHC, and DRY SOCKET.

In July 2025, they’ll join Tomar Control from Peru on tour, playing 10 concerts across 5 European countries — 3 of them for the first time.

Their mission? To make people move — dance, stage dive — while also raising awareness about political struggles and everyday resistance.
